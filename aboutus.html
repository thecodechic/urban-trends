about us

We are a fashion and style brand geared towards tending to the style needs of every urban woman.

Remember, if you can think it , you can wear it. Periodt!!!!

Urban trends started in 2020 when the COVID-19 pandemic was full blown in a self contained room in Abuja with just an instagram account. Today we have stores in Lagos,Owerri,Port-harcourt and Enugu with the Benin store in progress. We are a retail clothing store covering your clothing and accessories needs for your glam.
